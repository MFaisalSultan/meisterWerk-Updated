STANDARD FINISH: Solid Opaques, Textures, Metallics

Our opaque finishes are solid colors that are carefully curated. We aim to provide the most beautiful finishes. These solid colors come in a variety of shades and textures, such as glossy, matte, satin, and textured options.

PREMIUM FINISH: Polished Transparent Gloss & Satin Clears

Our premium, mirror-polished two-stage transparent finishes are meticulously crafted by combining high-luster ceramic-polished aluminum with multiple layers of top-quality clear coat. This process results in a visibly detailed and handcrafted finish.



(Available in Mirror Gloss Clear or Satin Anodized Clear)

PREMIUM FINISH: 120 Grit Brushed Transparent Gloss & Satin Clears

Our luxurious, brushed two-stage transparent finishes are meticulously crafted by combining 120-grit hand-brushed aluminum with multiple layers of high-quality transparent clear coat.



(Available in Gloss or Satin Clear)
